# Code of Conduct

HAVEN is built for older adults, families and care professionals. Contributors are expected to act with respect, patience and care.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing levels of technical experience.
- Prioritise elder dignity and safety.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Avoid dismissive or patronising language.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information.
- Shaming users or contributors.
- Treating accessibility, privacy or safety concerns as optional.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions or restrict participation when conduct conflicts with this code.
