# HAVEN Safety Copy Review

## Scam alert copy principles

- Calm, factual, no panic language.
- Never shame the elder.
- Never say a scam is certain unless the evidence threshold is high.
- Always offer a calm next action.

## Crisis phrase copy principles

- Acknowledge presence: "I am with you" / "Ik ben bij u".
- Notify family and show Help immediately.
- Do not over-promise clinical support.
- Emergency services remain available.

## AI transparency

Required first-use disclosure:

- EN: "Hello, I am HAVEN — your digital helper."
- NL: "Hallo, ik ben HAVEN — uw digitale hulp."

Banned deceptive copy:

- "I am a real employee"
- "I am not a computer"
- "You are speaking to a person"
