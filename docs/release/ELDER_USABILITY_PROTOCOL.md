# HAVEN Older-Adult Usability Protocol

## Participants

- Minimum 5 adults aged 68+.
- At least 2 participants with low digital literacy.
- At least 1 participant using assistive technology or enlarged text.

## Tasks

1. Open HAVEN and identify the Help button.
2. Confirm a medication reminder.
3. Ask HAVEN a voice question.
4. Read a family message.
5. Respond to an amber scam alert.
6. Review what family can see.

## Success criteria

- Participant can complete medication confirmation without coaching.
- Participant can find Help in under 10 seconds.
- Participant can explain in their own words what a scam alert is asking them to do.
- Participant does not describe the app as surveillance or patronising.

## Stop criteria

Any participant feels panicked, shamed or confused by an emergency/scam/medication flow; the flow must be redesigned before launch.
