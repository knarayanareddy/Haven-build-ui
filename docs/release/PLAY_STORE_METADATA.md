# HAVEN Play Store Metadata

## Short description
A calm elder companion for safety, medicine and family connection.

## Full description
HAVEN helps older adults stay safe, connected and independent with a voice-first interface designed for dignity. It includes medication reminders, family messages, scam protection, safe-zone support, life story recording and emergency medical profile access.

The elder controls consent. Family access is permission-scoped. Precise location is not shown to family and is automatically removed after the short safety window.

## Data safety notes
- No BSN collection.
- No advertising profile.
- No sale of personal data.
- Health and location features require explicit consent.
- Account deletion and data export are supported.
